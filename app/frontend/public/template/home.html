What is LetsDoHobby?

LetsDoHobby is a simple platform where you can find your hobby partner to enjoy your hobbies or any other activities that you love to do with local people.
The main idea is to take people from the social networks activities to real life activities
The second thing the app focus on is bringing like minded people together
There aren't a lot of fancy functionalities in there, it's very simple app

The main features of LetsDoHobby

  1. Personal Profile where people can see what you are good at or what you love to
  2. Feeds where you see people posting ads based on single activity, in this ad they describe when, where, and how many people they need to practice the selected activity
  3. The user can also post his own ad looking for the right partner for a specific activity
  4. If the user like the ad, a group chat opens automatically and anyone else likes the ad will be a member of this group chat, this way members can start arranging the meeting and some other needs for them self
  5. Meeting people with similar interests as yours in your area.
  6. User can see the ads based on the user's location range, which mean the user can not change his location, but he can decide the range of location
